From the Plains to the Peaks – Safari Adventures in Kenya

Rolfam Tours and Travel and Travel has been operating in Kenya with well-traveled guides and crew who have spent over a decade leading inbound tours and safaris across East Africa. Based on the coast in Mombasa, we specialise in tailored Kenyan journeys — from the great migration in the Maasai Mara, to the elephant herds of Amboseli under Kilimanjaro, the red dust of Tsavo, and the white sands of Diani.

Whether you're after a budget road safari, a fly-in lodge experience, or a hand-tailored honeymoon itinerary, our team handles every detail — transport, lodging, park fees, guides — so you can focus on the wild.
